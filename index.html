We’re A locally owned and operated business in downtown Coeur d'Alene serving coffee, tea and delicious treats.

Built in 1904, the EXCHANGE NATIONAL BANK of Coeur d’Alene has also served as an ice cream parlor, a pharmacy and even the town’s library (2nd floor) in the early 1900’s.

We’re proud to serve the many generations that gather on this corner for coffee, conversation and to create their own memories!
